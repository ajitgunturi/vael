Governing Principles
Built on five non-negotiable rules
When any two conflict, the one listed first wins. Privacy always comes first.
🔒

1. Privacy is non-negotiable

Data never leaves your device unencrypted. No telemetry, no analytics, no server-side processing. Big tech is storage, never audience.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

2. Family unit, individual boundaries

Shared budgets and combined net worth at the family level. But every member controls what they share. Selective visibility is first-class.

🧮

3. Deterministic over intelligent

Financial calculations use math and rules, not models. No AI/ML — on-device or cloud. PMT, FV, amortization — not predictions.

🇮🇳

4. India-native, globally coherent

Built for Indian banking formats, loan structures, and tax patterns. But extensible to other regions without rewrites.

💸

5. Self-contained, zero-cost

No backend servers. No subscriptions. No recurring fees. The app works fully offline. Your cost is zero beyond the device you own.

Security Architecture
Zero-knowledge by design
Your data is encrypted before it ever leaves your device. Even if Google scanned every file, they'd see only random noise.
  • 🔑

    AES-256-GCM Encryption

    Every piece of financial data encrypted with authenticated encryption before cloud sync.

  • 🧬

    PBKDF2 Key Derivation

    Family Encryption Key derived from your passphrase with 100,000 iterations of PBKDF2-SHA256.

  • 🗄️

    SQLCipher at Rest

    Local database encrypted with SQLCipher. Data is protected even if someone accesses your device storage.

  • 👻

    Opaque Cloud Storage

    File names on Drive are random hashes. No semantic leakage — no budget.json or salary.csv.

  • 🚫

    No Attack Surface

    Zero backend servers, APIs, or network endpoints. Nothing to breach because there's nothing to attack.
