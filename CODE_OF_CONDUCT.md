# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community and project

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated, and will result in a response deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances.
